Solar Homes



Another great attraction that could be found all over in many parts are Solar homes. As the electricity demand is increasing every year, a better solution to this is switching to renemwable energy resource. The residents who live in areas with adequate supply of sunlight have equipped their houses with solar panels which helps them generate their own energy. This type of green energy also lowers their carbon footprint. There is net metering which also allows them to sell back excess energy produced. They are also eligible for certain tax breaks. Not only is solar power good for the environment, but you can earn money selling back excess power to the grid. This has led to installation of solar power systems in nearly every household.
