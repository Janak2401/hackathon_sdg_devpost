Energy Park



To fulfill the ever increasing demand of the electricity, energy parks have been established. This energy parks generate electricity which is use as the main source of energy generation throughout the city. Along with that, the park has effected in producing job oppurtunities for the people living in the city. The gardens in the park gave a peaceful look. The Energy park encourage for people to come and produce off-grid electricity just by walking around on the specially design tiles. Hence, many people looking for a solitary atmosphere often come and walk around in the clean air. Careful implementation of the energy park concept has provided benefits beyond routine economic development, including Environmental sustainability, Potential R&D for the next generation of energy production and Integrated approach to energy production, distribution, storage and utilization.
