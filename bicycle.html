Bicycle Lanes



The city promotes the people to use bicycles as an alternative to automobiles. Long Bicycle lanes have been createad throughout the city for people to travel. Bicycle Parks are established at various places for people to park and use authority granted bicycles for public transport. These bicycle tracks also help in maintaining the health of the people of the city. Because of the friendly bicycle culture on these lanes people often choose bicycles for automobiles to commute between places. This reduces the heavy traffic and the chances of jam filled roads. Relatively low emission of the harmful gases from the automobiles also helps in the reduction of air pollution.
